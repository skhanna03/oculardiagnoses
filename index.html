Our Revolutionary Method

Diagnosing ADHD on a New Level

Increased Accuracy

Current diagnosis relies on unobjective, qualitative observations, which has led to historically inaccurate and flawed diagnoses. Our application utilizes an objective biomarker as the basis for diagnosing ADHD, yielding an accuracy of 94%.

Read More

Increased Efficiency

Current methods to diagnose ADHD require examinations which span multiple hours. The time from initial concern to a formal diagnosis can extend well past 13 months. Our application administers a 30 minute visuospatial memory test, carefully gathering and analyzing data to output accurate results.

Read More

Increased Accessibility

Our application, which can be run on a laptop or mobile phone, breaks the socio-economic barriers prevalent in ADHD diagnosis, allowing minorities and socio-economically disadvantaged groups to get the diagnosis and treatment regimen they need.

Read More

Eye Movements

Through extensive research, we determined that pupillary responses to visual stimuli could be utilized as an objective biomarker to diagnose ADHD.

Machine Learning

We derived a mechanism to analyze pupillometric variation in patients using a complex machine learning-based mathematical algorithm to analyze the data. Furthermore, we uncovered new behavioral understandings of ADHD in our preliminary machine learning analysis of pupil dynamics.

Applications to the Real World

More than a diagnostic tool, our application can simply be used as a medical advisement tool to provide the most comprehensive overview of pupillary biometrics in relation to having ADHD, as well as further steps to take to see a neurologist or medical practitioner.

How it Works

Redefining Neurobehavioral Understandings

Using proprietary deep learning algorithms that capture the pupil in real-time, our application analyzes variation in pupillary responses to visual stimuli, administering a visuospatial working memory task to output a probability / risk score of being diagnosed with ADHD, as well as further steps and medical advice. Underlying our application is a complex machine learning algorithm we developed to intelligently analyze pupil dynamics.
